About Us

Cobalt AI was founded by entrepreneurial business lawyers, knowledge management professionals and software engineers who are committed to the success of their clients.

Cobalt AI's team has in-depth experience in conducting a wide range of legal knowledge projects including:

  • Large-scale HR policy review
  • Large-scale M&A roll-up playbook creation
  • International commercial negotiations (MSAs) at-scale
  • RFP and procurement document organization
  • Large-scale review and comparisons of securities documents

The Cobalt AI team has conducted a multitude of other lawyer and technology-combined projects, creating a smart, efficient and thorough client deliverable experience.
