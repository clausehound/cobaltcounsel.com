Read and Learn

Clausehound's Document Library is a cloud-based document drafting environment that addresses knowledge gaps in first instance document-drafting, and replaces manual research and writing with an automated process. The library hosts tens of thousands of legal clauses, contracts, news or blog articles, lawyer commentary and instructional videos. Read and learn using our lawyer-vetted contract templates, carefully crafted to help your business succeed! 

Ask

If you come across any questions or concerns during the drafting process, Clausehound's Knowledge Helpdesk will direct you to our ever-growing blog of learning materials. Where your questions require legal advice, our learning materials will help you frame your questions for your supporting lawyers at the firm.

Prepare

Once you have had a chance to read the learning materials, edit the documents from the library and annotate with your notes and comments, including learning materials copied directly from our library, to give the supporting lawyer the context for your editing decisions.
